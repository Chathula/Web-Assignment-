Welcome to NSBM Green University of Sri Lanka

Breaking the ground rules of higher education, NSBM has initiated the construction of Sri Lanka’s first ever Green University Township making Sri Lanka one of the main educational hubs in the world. Inspired with the vision to make Sri Lanka the ‘Knowledge Hub’ of Asia, NSBM is geared with exploring innovativeness in teaching, learning, research and extracurricular activities facilitated by the remarkable infrastructure that will be completed in 2015.

NSBM Green University Town is totally handled by the Government of Sri Lanka like a state University in the country. Watch the following video to get a quick picture.
